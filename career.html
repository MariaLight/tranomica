Values

Volunteering

Volunteering is an integral part of our philosophy at TRANOMICA. We encourage and support volunteer activities of our employees, realizing that they can benefit society and help those who need support.

Education

At TRANOMICA we attach great importance to education and development. We provide opportunities for professional growth for our employees by providing access to education, training and development programs. We also support educational initiatives in our communities by providing financial support or resources to educational institutions. We believe that investing in education and development is key to the success of not only our employees, but also society as a whole.

Care

Caring for our employees is one of our core values at TRANOMICA. We create a supportive work environment where employees can realize their potential and achieve personal and professional goals. We promote work-life balance, provide health and wellness programs, and invest in developing the leadership and interpersonal skills of our employees.

Culture

We also attach great importance to the development and preservation of culture. We are committed to working with local organizations and institutions to support and promote the cultural diversity and creativity of our communities.

Work with us

Developed corporate culture. In addition to the system of mentoring and professional training, we pay special attention to improving the internal atmosphere of the team.

Career growth and individual contribution. In our company there are opportunities for professional growth and the implementation of your ideas, which can qualitatively influence the vector of business development.

Registration according to the Labor Code. VHI and compensation for education, gym.
