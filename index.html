Our experts have many years of experience in these areas, which is confirmed by the high quality of our services, effective problem-solving, and a growing number of clients and partners around the world.

Our areas

Logistics

TRANOMICA provides cargo transportation using logistics infrastructure including sea, air, rail and road transportation. Thanks to our own customs agents we ensure the accuracy and timeliness of deliveries.

Finance

TRANOMICA provides alternative services to classical banking thanks to its financial institution. Partners receive income as suppliers: the company offers favorable price conditions for the purchase and sale of commodities.

Analytics

Monitoring and analysis of key market indicators and global events lead to reasonable decision-making, development of effective strategies and enhancement of the strategic advantages that ensure the qualitative growth of the TRANOMICA group of companies.

ESG

The company is a responsible participant in the financial market. TRANOMICA is committed to high standards of corporate governance, creating equal growth opportunities for all employees. The company aims for transparency by providing customers with clear information about activities and results.
